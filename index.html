Scope


Recently, autonomous systems play a crucial role in industrial applications to smart cities. However, the emergence of new technologies and its applications push the need for more research and development in the domain of autonomous systems to make it more user-independent and reliable. The recent advances in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Deep Learning enable autonomous systems to increase its flexibility and robustness. This International Conference on Reinforcement Learning for Autonomous Systems (ICRLAS 2024) is dedicated to explore the recent research innovations in the areas of deep learning and autonomous systems. The theme for ICRLAS “Autonomous Reinforcement Learning”, focusing the need for designing, developing and implementing new algorithms and simulated environments and implement them in real-world autonomous systems.
